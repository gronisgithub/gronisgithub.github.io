Liechtenstein, Vaduz, 2001-12-29.

 

The tiny country of Liechtenstein. This was one of the first things I saw, when I was going to Vaduz (the capital of Liechtenstein).

A few days before new year, it's very calm in this small capital.





A castle that is portrayed on many postcards, so it must be something important in Liechtenstein.

Very small and charming 'city'. A nice place to be a tourist for one day.
