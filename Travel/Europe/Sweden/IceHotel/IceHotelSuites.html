One of the best sculptures. The bed is decorated of ice, like a Viking ship. Highly appreciated.

If you wonder where the light-switch is to turn off the light when you go to bed, I can tell you that there is none. Once inside the sleeping bag, you try to only have a breathing hole. If the hole is too large, you will freeze very quiclky.

It's a challenge to sleep here, no doubt about this. Especially for the Japanese tourists that where in the hotel the same time as I was. I thought it was tough, but they had a lot worse time.
