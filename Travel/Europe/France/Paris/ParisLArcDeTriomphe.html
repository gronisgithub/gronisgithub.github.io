France, Paris, 2001-07.

L'Arc De Triomphe.

At the start of Champs Elysées.

Looks solid from outside, but not so solid when you are going up on the roof.



This was the first thing i recognized when I arrived in Paris. The height of it, and thickness was impressive. After looking at it, and it's sculptures I started to look at the 6 lane round about. I soon understood that the round about works in a complete different way in France than in Sweden. People going out of the round about, has to break for the people entering the round about. The exact opposite of Sweden. But they managed quite well. That was the beginning of my observations on the differences between France and Sweden.
