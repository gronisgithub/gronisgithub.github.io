France, Paris, 2001-05.


No page about Paris is complete without a photo of the Eiffel Tower. So here it is. What surprised me the most was the size. It is really big. A clearly visible land mark. And getting up there took it's time. One hour queuing, and 20 minutes at least to get up to the top.







It's huge!

Many lights are flashing a on this tower. It's totally covered with light flashing in an irregular pattern.

Paris by night, with a view from the Eiffel Tower. You see the river Seine playing it's part in the scenery of Paris.
