France, Paris, 2001-05.


The Louvre.

It takes a while, just to get in.

Afterwards I discovered that there are other entrances, that had much less queues.

Outside, they had an "Arc De Triomphe", which was much smaller than the original one.

If you take a ride in the Ferris wheel, you see a long way. You also discover that the Louvre is really big.
