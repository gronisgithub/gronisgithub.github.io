Thomson Gazelle.

Hey. Look. Cheetah food.
We have all seen the nature shows where the cheetah is hunting the small Thomson gazelle, and catching it. But they survive in numbers. Enough to withstand cheetah attacks, and few enough to feed on the gras.

This little fellow looks quite scared, and nervous in it's step. Let's hope that it survives the day. And have a nice day for that matter.
