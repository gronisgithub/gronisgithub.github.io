Elephant.

I've seen live elephants before. But not out in their natural environment. They get huge. It would be no match for them to throw the car I was in, wherever they felt like.

This friendly animal has respect everywhere it goes. This is the true king of the globe. Nothing will threaten it (except us humans with our guns). An animal that breaks down trees in a matter of minutes, using brute force, gives a great deal of respect.

Lions and other small animals always keep away from the elephant, as you never know what an elephant might be thinking. If it decides to attack, there is no where to hide. The only way is to run for your life, or in this case drive away.

But they look so friendly, when standing there, observing you.
