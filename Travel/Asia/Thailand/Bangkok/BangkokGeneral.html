Thailand, Bangkok, 1999-08

This was my first stop in Asia, and my first real contact with the Thai people. The weather in Bangkok was quite good, since I like it hot. I'm from Sweden - what do you expect? The weather in Sweden is cold and windy. Compared to Thailand's weather which is warm, humid and still. The exact opposite of Swedish weather.

The people where just as friendly as I had imagined (since my visit in Phuket). And the food is just as good. Actually, the food is really good all over Asia. Lots of different new things to try. Although I did not want to try pig stomach etc. My recommendation is to try all the local food you can. You've probably heard this over and over, but - I think that experiencing the food in all its variations will give you a greater experience of the country.

Shopping for fake things in Thailand is famous. It's so cheap. Things you should buy in Thailand: T-shirts, ladies dresses, belts, wristwatches. You can almost always get the thing you want for less than half the price they ask for. Normally between 30-50% of the original price. How to get the price down is up to you.

The first time I saw Star wars Episode I was at The Emporium in Bangkok. The theatre was one of my all time favorites. Huge screen, incredible sound system and just the right price. That's to say, the right price for me; the locals think that this is really expensive, but to us Europeans it's cheap. I would have spent a lot more to see Episode I. And since it was so cheap and excellent theatre, I decided to see it again, 2 days later here.
Which other movies did I see in Asia?? None.
