Beijing, 1999-08

China is wonderful. I have to say as some of my friends: Once you've been to China, you miss it and want to return.
 



If you want to go shopping for Clothes, go to Silk Market. If you want clothes, CD and DVD go to Bar Street. The CDs are 7 RMB/piece (1$). DVDs are 35 RMB. Expect that 10-15% of the DVDs/CDs will not work when you get home. Some of the DVDs are also in poor quality. There is no way of telling in advance.

I can recommend a great tour guide: Helen. The company she works for is called Panda Tour. But I suggest that you take a tour where she can guide you. Her English is excellent.
