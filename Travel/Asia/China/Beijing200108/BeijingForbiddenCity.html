China, Beijing, Forbidden City, 2001-08

Located at the far end of Thiamin Square, you find The Forbidden City. Luckily it's not forbidden for us tourists to visit now days.

As I was here a few years earlier, I'm writing some other comments about this city now.
 

This is part of the old wall surrounding the Forbidden City.

Looking at The Forbidden City from the entrance, doesn't look that spectacular.
The building you se on this picture, just looks like an ordinary traditional Chinese building.

I then realize how huge this wall really is, as I walk through it. The building you see on the previous picture, is actually standing on this wall.
This wall is not something you break through, the first time you try. (Not the following one hundred times either.)



Walking thru many walls, with important buildings on them, seems to be more of a rule, than an exceptoion here. Is this The Forbidden City? Everything is just built on these big red walls?

Well, here's something different.

If you think that my "tour" of the city is a bit boring, you should go here yourself, and rent the audio tour. It's Roger Moore giving you a tour via your rented freestyle. I would recommend it, if you choose to go here without a guided tour (as I had last time).



The more I see of The Forbidden City, the larger it becomes for me. There's the Museum of Watches, and some other points of interest.

The buildings are generally very large here. Compare to the humans walking over by this building.



The stairs that the Emperor was carried over. This was created out of a single "rock". No room for mistakes here.

As a member of today's society, I ask my self: Was it really worth it to create this stone for the Emperor to be carried over? He never walked here on foot any way.
 



This is where the water was stored, incase there was a fire. And as the buildings are of wood, there where some major incidents.

Every year, the Emperor would add another layer of gold to these very large pots. And they where plenty too.
When the soldiers came here the first time, they cut much of the gold away, with every mean possible. Greed will do that.



The eternal defender. He always watch over the building behind him, preventing evil people to enter.
On the opposite side of the door, there's another one of these, but female, and having her paw on on a sphere instead.
