About Grönis; Character


I like to party. My way of partying is shared with a lot of other Swedes - to drink alcohol together with lots of friends. After a while, I normally want to go out to dance. For some reason I find that fun to do, but I have trouble doing this when I am sober (or almost sober).

I learned to dance Hip-hop when I was 21, on a dancing school in Växjö. I was by far the oldest one in my class, but I found it to be fun. I quit after finding our that the next step would be to practice the steps so be able to compete in some small tournaments. I figured that I have reached my goal then, and I was finally able to go out and dance on discos, without feeling nervous for it. That's right - I had only been to one "real" disco before that, and that was just before transferring to the University of Växjö. This course really helped my self confidence.
Pretty similar to the improvement of my self confidence that my Ju-jitsu training did for me. I got my black belt in May 1990. These 2 together helped to shape the person I am today.
After my black belt in Ju-jitsu, I took orange or green belt in Judo. I won a few small contests in Judo, but this was not my thing. Too much competition. I enjoyed throwing people around though.

Taking pictures with my camera, is also something I enjoy. Perhaps I will buy a digital camera one day, when the price is right for a camera that can produce 3000x2000 pixels. This camera should also be able to use my current lenses.

I don't like to drive cars, but for some reason I like to drive my motorcycle. In 1999, I really wanted to buy a blue Yamaha YZF R1.

These days I usually wear Boss Elements, which I buy at Kastrup (Copenhagen Airport). A friend of mine asked me one day which perfume I was using, and I did not realize that I smell that much. After a while we came to the conclusion that it might be the perfume itself has a very strong fragrance. That's what friends are for, make you realize things about yourself that only they can tell you.
