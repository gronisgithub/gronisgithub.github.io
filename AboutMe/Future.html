About Grönis; Future

I like to travel to lots of different places. One of the places I would love to go is to the far northern part of Sweden in winter to see the northern lights.

To continue on the northern theme. Svalbard on Greenland at winter/spring to watch these magnificent polar bears. In the local language the name "Polar bear" is directly translated to the "eternal walker". As the name, the polar bear almost never stays at the same place. They have to keep moving to find food. Needless to say, the polar bear covers great distances every day, more than any man ever could.
Imagine my feelings when I saw that they have polar bears at zoos. The bears cant roam around in the open like they use to. They have to stay in that cage for many years at least.
The eternal walker locked in a cage. Just like a convict. Which crime did these bears commit?

After going to the Antarctica, I have trouble finding another place that I would like to visit. Perhaps Galapagos.
