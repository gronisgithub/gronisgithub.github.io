About Grönis, Music

My favourite group is La Bouche. I try to get all their songs, and all possible re-mixes, on CD (or on mp3).
Another group that I listen a lot to is Basic Element.
Mostly I listen to Euro-disco, which is sort of like club-music or Techno.

I tend to try and listen to music constantly at home. As soon as I get home from work, I turn on either the stereo, or the computer or both.

You could say that I'm a slave under the top dance-charts, and I wouldn't call you a liar. I might in fact agree with you. The kind of music they play at the dance charts in Sweden is the kind of music I listen to. But there's only room for a certain amount of music on the charts. So I, like everyone else, have to find my music somewhere else too. I download a lot of Euro-dance, and generally they where produced around 1997 +-5 years.
