About Grönis; Movies, DVD

Movies are a big part of my life (but becoming less and less important).
Since the release of DVD, I've become a big fan of collecting DVD movies. The pirate-DVDs in China are very cheap, so I bought a lot of them when I was there. Enough to fill one suite case, all by them selves. Now you see how much I used to like to watch movies.
It really helps me to keep my English alive. Whenever possible I watch the movie without any subtitles to get the true feeling of the movies, the way it was intended to be seen.

In surround sound, and DVD quality gives you a good impression of how serious my movie watching is.

My favorite movie is Leon (The Professional in the US). 
The movie I've seen the most times is the Labyrinth. I've seen this one just over 100 times. Needless to say, I have it on DVD and VHS.
Some summer nights I've spent watching 2-3 movies every single night. This was mostly when I was living with my grandparents for some weeks. After work, there is only this many things you can do, so I had time to develop my interest for movies.
