About Grönis; A small story I made up

Once upon a time there was a tiny IP-address called 192.71.126.154. But she was so tiny and cute that all of her friends just called her Pingeling. Whenever they wanted to make a joke about her name they called her ICMP II Echo Request.

One day she was running around in the big net. Happy to get an invitation from 185.24.243.172 (that she always referred to as her "big hundredeightyfive twentyfour friend") she wanted to go to his party that he was having in a buffer somewhere. After traveling a while, she came cross a big nasty Router. The bad router did not want to let her through to the other interface. Sad, she sat down on a lost packet and tried to find a way to get to her friend.

Then she got an idea. What if she timed out, then perhaps another router would help her find her way. So she timed out.

Then she found a lot of friendly routers that wanted to help her on her way. She promised to come visit them after she had been to the party. Finally she arrived at his party, and it was a great party. They danced and talked about the clever IP version 6 that had joined them. He told then about the new world opening, and about the BFR (Cisco 12000) he had chatted with on his way here. They promised to meet here again in one month.
